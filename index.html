Do you remember Stunt Car Racer from 1989? It was available on Amiga, Commodore 64, and other computers at the time. If you don't, consider watching a longplay video.

Someone got hold of the original Amiga source code, converted it to C++, and put it on SourceForge. I then replaced the DirectX calls with OpenGL calls and compiled it with emscripten to get HTML, JavaScript, and WebAssembly. GitHub Copilot helped me add a tournament system, two-player mode, game menus, and more.

If you want the technical details, read my blog posts!
